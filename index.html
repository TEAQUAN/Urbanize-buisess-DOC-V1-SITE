Who we are

Urbanize is a lifestyle Tech and entertainment company that focuses on providing Urban solutions to problems faced in the lifestyle sector while curating and creating entertaining content for Urban Africans.

Our mission

Our mission is to enhance and improve the quality of life of our audience by providing products, services, and experiences that align with their values, interests, and aspirations. We aim to create a brand identity that resonates with our target audience and to build a community of people who share common values and interests. We want to offer high-quality and innovative products, develop sustainable and ethical practices, and create engaging and immersive experiences that inspire and delight customers. Ultimately, we aim to provide customers with a holistic and integrated approach to their lives and to empower them to live their best life possible.

Our Goal:

To create an Urban lifestyle like never before through storytelling and entertainment. The World is moving at a very fast pace and we want Africa to be at the forefront of the Urban lifestyle.

Target audience:

Gen Z and Millenials (Ages 16-40)

Problem's we have encoutered

Fierce competition: The lifestyle industry is highly competitive, with many companies vying for the attention and loyalty of consumers. This can make it difficult for new or smaller companies to gain a foothold in the market.

Economic downturns: Economic downturns can impact consumer spending habits, which can have a significant impact on lifestyle companies. Companies that rely heavily on discretionary spending may be particularly vulnerable during times of economic uncertainty.

Shifting consumer preferences: Consumer preferences for lifestyle products and services can be fickle, and companies that fail to keep up with changing trends may struggle to remain relevant.

Supply chain disruptions: The lifestyle industry relies on global supply chains to source materials and produce products. Supply chain disruptions, such as those caused by natural disasters or geopolitical conflicts, can impact production and lead to product shortages or delays.

Brand reputation: Lifestyle companies rely heavily on their brand reputation and image to attract and retain customers. Negative publicity or public relations scandals can significantly damage a company's reputation and impact sales
